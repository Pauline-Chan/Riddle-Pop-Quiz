Q1: What two things can you never eat for breakfast?

A: Lunch and dinner.

Q2: What has hands and a face, but can't hold anything or smile?

A: A clock

Q3: Bobby throws a ball as hard as he can. It comes back to him, even though nothing and nobody touches it. How?

A: He throws it straight up

Q4: Why is an island like the letter 'T'?

A: Because it is in the middle of water

Q5: How do you make the number one disappear?

A: Add the letter ‘G’ and it’s gone

Q6: Name three days consecutively where none of the seven days of the week appear.

A: Yesterday, today, and tomorrow

Q7: Who is the fastest runner in the whole world?

A: Adam. He was the first in the human race.

Q8: What Question can you never answer 'Yes' to?

A: Are you asleep yet?

Q9: What is the centre of gravity?

A: The letter 'V'

Q10: Jack rode into town on Friday and rode out two days later on Friday. How can that be possible?

A: Friday is his horse's name

Q11: What is always in front of you but can't be seen?

A: The future

Q12: What did the beach say when the tide came in?

A: Long time, no sea!

Q13: What kind of cup can't hold water ?

A: A cupcake

Q14: What has many rings, but no fingers?

A: A telephone

Q15: Why isn't your nose twelve inches long?

A: Because then it would be a foot

Q16: What did the outlaw get when he stole a calendar?

A: Twelve months

Q17: What bank never has any money?

A: The riverbank

Q18: A man was driving his truck. His lights were not on. The moon was not out. Up ahead, a woman was crossing the street. How did he see her?

A: It was a bright and sunny day

Q19: What can't talk but will reply when spoken to?

A: An echo

Q20: What is as light as a feather, but even the world's strongest man couldn't hold it for more than a minute?

A: His breath

Q21: The more of this there is, the less you see.

A: Darkness

Q22: Two fathers and two sons go on a fishing trip. They each catch a fish and bring it home. Why do they only bring three home?

A: The fishing trip consists of a grandfather, a father, and a son.

Q23: What instrument can you hear but never see?

A: Your voice! You can sing with your voice like an instrument and hear it, but no one can see it.

Q24: I have keys but no locks. I have space but no room. You can enter but can't go outside. What am I?

A: A keyboard

Q25: One night, a butcher, a baker, and a candlestick maker go to a hotel. When they get their bill, however, it's for four people. Who's the fourth person?

A: One night can also mean one knight. That makes four – one knight, one butcher, a baker, and a candlestick maker!
